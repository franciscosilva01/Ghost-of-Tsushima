About the game

The game is set at the end of the 12th century, when the Mongol Empire destroyed nations to conquer the East, and all that remains between Japan and the Mongol invasion is the island of Tsushima. That's where Jin Sakai is, determined to do whatever it takes to protect his home. By waging war for the freedom of Tshushima, Jin becomes known as Ghost.
